# Patient Billing

## Overview
Comprehensive patient billing system managing statement generation, payment reminders, payment plans, and collections with multi-language support and patient-friendly interfaces.

## Key Components

### Statement Generation
- **Automated Statements**: Scheduled billing statement generation and delivery
- **Multi-language Support**: Billing statements in Hebrew and English
- **Customizable Templates**: Practice-branded statement templates and layouts
- **Electronic Delivery**: Email and portal-based statement delivery options

### Payment Reminders
- **Automated Reminders**: Multi-channel payment reminder campaigns
- **Escalation Workflows**: Progressive reminder sequences for overdue accounts
- **Personalized Messaging**: Customized reminder content based on patient preferences
- **Delivery Tracking**: Reminder delivery confirmation and response tracking

### Payment Plans
- **Flexible Terms**: Customizable payment plan options and terms
- **Automatic Setup**: AI-powered payment plan recommendations based on account balance
- **Payment Tracking**: Real-time payment plan progress and compliance monitoring
- **Default Management**: Automated handling of missed payments and plan modifications

### Patient Communication
- **Billing Inquiries**: Dedicated channels for billing questions and disputes
- **Financial Counseling**: Patient financial counseling and assistance programs
- **Hardship Programs**: Special payment arrangements for financial hardship cases
- **Transparency Tools**: Cost estimates and financial responsibility calculators

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Automated billing process reducing administrative costs by 50%
- ✅ Improved patient payment rates through flexible payment options
- ✅ Multi-language billing support for diverse patient populations
- ✅ Enhanced patient satisfaction through transparent and flexible billing practices
