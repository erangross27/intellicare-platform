# Regulatory Compliance Training

## Overview
Mandatory regulatory compliance training program ensuring all staff understand and comply with healthcare regulations, safety requirements, and legal obligations.

## Key Components

### Regulatory Curriculum
- **Healthcare Regulations**: Israel Ministry of Health regulations and compliance requirements
- **International Standards**: WHO guidelines and international healthcare standards
- **Safety Protocols**: Occupational safety and infection control procedures
- **Legal Requirements**: Healthcare law and professional liability requirements

### Compliance Tracking
- **Mandatory Training**: Required training completion for all staff categories
- **Certification Tracking**: Professional certification and license compliance monitoring
- **Renewal Management**: Automated renewal tracking and notification systems
- **Exception Handling**: Management of training compliance exceptions and remediation

### Multi-jurisdictional Support
- **Israeli Regulations**: Local healthcare regulations and Ministry of Health requirements
- **International Compliance**: Support for US and other international market entry
- **Language Support**: Training materials in Hebrew and English
- **Cultural Adaptation**: Culturally appropriate training content and delivery

### Assessment and Validation
- **Knowledge Testing**: Comprehensive testing of regulatory knowledge and understanding
- **Competency Validation**: Practical demonstration of regulatory compliance procedures
- **Regular Assessment**: Ongoing evaluation of regulatory knowledge currency
- **Remedial Training**: Additional training for compliance gaps and deficiencies

## Success Criteria
- ✅ 100% staff completion of mandatory regulatory training
- ✅ Current knowledge of all applicable healthcare regulations
- ✅ Multi-jurisdictional compliance capability for international operations
- ✅ Systematic compliance monitoring and gap remediation
