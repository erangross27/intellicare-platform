# Staff Training Programs

## Overview
Comprehensive HIPAA training and certification program ensuring all healthcare staff understand and comply with privacy and security regulations.

## Key Components

### Training Curriculum
- **HIPAA Fundamentals**: Core privacy and security rule requirements
- **PHI Handling**: Proper procedures for protected health information
- **Breach Response**: Incident detection and response procedures
- **Role-Specific Training**: Customized training based on job function and access level

### Certification Management
- **Initial Certification**: New employee onboarding requirements
- **Annual Recertification**: Yearly training and testing requirements
- **Continuing Education**: Ongoing education for regulation updates
- **Competency Assessment**: Skills validation and performance measurement

### Implementation Integration
- **Service**: `securityTrainingService.js` - Complete training management
- **Models**: `TrainingRecord.js` - Training history and certification tracking
- **Scheduling**: Automated training reminders and deadline enforcement
- **Reporting**: Compliance reporting for training requirements

### Training Delivery
- **Multi-language Support**: Hebrew and English training materials
- **Interactive Modules**: Engaging training content with assessments
- **Mobile Access**: Training available on mobile devices
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time training progress and completion monitoring

## Success Criteria
- ✅ 100% staff completion of required HIPAA training
- ✅ Automated training delivery and tracking system
- ✅ Multi-language training materials for diverse workforce
- ✅ Comprehensive reporting for regulatory compliance
