# Referral Request System

## Overview
Comprehensive referral management system enabling seamless patient transfers between providers, departments, and external specialists with full tracking and communication.

## Key Components

### Referral Creation
- **Request Form**: Digital referral form with patient information, reason, urgency level
- **Provider Selection**: Internal provider directory and external specialist network
- **Documentation**: Medical history, test results, and relevant patient documents
- **Priority Handling**: Urgent, routine, elective referral categorization

### Workflow Management
- **Status Tracking**: Requested, Approved, Scheduled, Completed referral states
- **Approval Process**: Multi-level approval for specialist and external referrals
- **Authorization**: Insurance pre-authorization integration and tracking
- **Timeline Management**: Referral deadlines and follow-up scheduling

### Communication Hub
- **Provider Notifications**: Real-time alerts to referring and receiving providers
- **Patient Updates**: Automated status notifications via preferred channels
- **Documentation Sharing**: Secure transfer of medical records and test results
- **Inter-practice Messaging**: Encrypted communication between healthcare facilities

### Integration Points
- **EHR Integration**: Patient record synchronization and update mechanisms
- **Insurance Systems**: Real-time eligibility and authorization checking
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Automatic appointment booking with referred providers
- **Billing Coordination**: Referral billing and cost-sharing management

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Streamlined referral process reducing administrative overhead
- ✅ Real-time tracking and communication between all parties
- ✅ Automated insurance authorization and scheduling
- ✅ Complete audit trail for referral management compliance
