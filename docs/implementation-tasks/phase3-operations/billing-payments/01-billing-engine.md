# Billing Engine

## Overview
Comprehensive billing system managing charge capture, claim generation, payment processing, and revenue cycle management for multi-tenant medical practice operations.

## Key Components

### Charge Capture
- **Service Documentation**: Automatic CPT code assignment based on appointment types
- **Diagnosis Linking**: ICD-10 diagnosis code integration with billing charges
- **Modifier Application**: Appropriate modifier assignment for billing accuracy
- **Time-based Billing**: Duration-based charging for consultation and procedure codes

### Claim Generation
- **Electronic Claims**: Automated CMS-1500 and UB-04 claim generation
- **Batch Processing**: Bulk claim submission and processing workflows
- **Real-time Validation**: Claim validation before submission to reduce denials
- **Multi-payer Support**: Integration with various insurance payers and clearinghouses

### Revenue Cycle Management
- **Account Receivables**: Outstanding balance tracking and aging reports
- **Payment Posting**: Automatic payment posting from insurance and patient payments
- **Denial Management**: Systematic denial tracking and resubmission workflows
- **Collection Activities**: Automated patient billing and collection processes

### Financial Reporting
- **Revenue Analytics**: Practice revenue tracking and performance metrics
- **Payer Analysis**: Insurance payer performance and reimbursement analysis
- **Provider Productivity**: Individual provider billing and collection metrics
- **Cost Center Reporting**: Department and service line financial performance

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Automated billing process reducing manual intervention by 80%
- ✅ Clean claim rate above 95% through validation and compliance checking
- ✅ Revenue cycle optimization with reduced days in accounts receivable
- ✅ Comprehensive financial reporting for practice management decisions
