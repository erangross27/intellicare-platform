# Waitlist Management

## Overview
Dynamic waitlist system automatically matching cancelled appointments with waiting patients and optimizing practice capacity utilization.

## Key Components

### Waitlist Queue
- **Priority System**: Urgent, routine appointment prioritization
- **FIFO Processing**: First-in-first-out for same priority levels
- **Provider Specific**: Separate waitlists per provider/specialist
- **Date Range Flexibility**: Patient available date range preferences

### Automatic Matching
- **Cancellation Triggers**: Instant waitlist processing on cancellations
- **Real-time Notifications**: Immediate patient contact for available slots
- **Preference Matching**: Provider, time, date preference alignment
- **Multi-channel Alerts**: SMS, Email, Push notifications for matches

### Patient Preferences
- **Time Windows**: Preferred appointment time ranges
- **Provider Selection**: Specific provider or "any available" options
- **Urgency Levels**: Medical priority-based queue positioning
- **Contact Methods**: Notification channel preferences

### Queue Management
- **Position Tracking**: Real-time waitlist position updates
- **Expiration Handling**: Automatic removal of expired requests
- **Response Timeouts**: Time limits for patient responses to offers
- **Alternative Offers**: Secondary options when first choice declined

### Analytics Integration
- **Waitlist Metrics**: Average wait times, fulfillment rates
- **Demand Forecasting**: Capacity planning based on waitlist data
- **Provider Utilization**: Efficiency metrics for appointment allocation
- **Patient Satisfaction**: Waitlist experience tracking

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Automated matching of cancelled slots with waiting patients
- ✅ Real-time notifications within minutes of availability
- ✅ Maximum practice capacity utilization
- ✅ Fair queue management with priority handling
