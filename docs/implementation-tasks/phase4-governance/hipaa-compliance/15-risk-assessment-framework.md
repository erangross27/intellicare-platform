# Risk Assessment Framework

## Overview
Comprehensive risk assessment framework providing continuous evaluation of HIPAA compliance risks, vulnerabilities, and security controls effectiveness.

## Key Components

### Risk Identification
- **Threat Modeling**: Systematic identification of potential threats to PHI
- **Vulnerability Assessment**: Regular vulnerability scanning and assessment
- **Asset Inventory**: Complete inventory of PHI-containing systems and processes
- **Risk Register**: Centralized risk register with impact and likelihood ratings

### Assessment Methodology
- **Quantitative Analysis**: Numerical risk scoring using industry-standard frameworks
- **Qualitative Evaluation**: Expert-based risk assessment and professional judgment
- **Control Effectiveness**: Security control testing and effectiveness measurement
- **Third-Party Assessment**: Independent risk assessment and validation

### Implementation Integration
- **Compliance Scorecard**: Real-time risk scoring through `complianceScorecard.js`
- **Security Monitoring**: Integration with security monitoring and threat detection
- **Audit Integration**: Risk assessment integration with audit and compliance systems
- **Reporting Dashboard**: Executive risk reporting and visualization

### Continuous Monitoring
- **Automated Assessment**: System-driven continuous risk evaluation
- **Periodic Reviews**: Regular comprehensive risk assessment cycles
- **Change Assessment**: Risk evaluation for system and process changes
- **Trend Analysis**: Risk trend identification and predictive analysis

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Comprehensive identification and assessment of all HIPAA-related risks
- ✅ Continuous monitoring with real-time risk visibility
- ✅ Integration with compliance monitoring and security systems
- ✅ Data-driven risk management and mitigation planning
