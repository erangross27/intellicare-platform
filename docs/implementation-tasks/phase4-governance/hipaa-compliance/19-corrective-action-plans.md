# Corrective Action Plans

## Overview
Comprehensive corrective action management system addressing HIPAA compliance violations, audit findings, and security incidents through systematic remediation planning and tracking.

## Key Components

### Violation Response
- **Automated Detection**: Real-time identification of compliance violations and deviations
- **Impact Assessment**: Systematic assessment of violation severity and potential impact
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Comprehensive investigation of underlying causes and contributing factors
- **Remediation Planning**: Development of targeted corrective action plans

### Action Management
- **Priority Classification**: Risk-based prioritization of corrective actions
- **Resource Allocation**: Assignment of responsible parties and required resources
- **Timeline Management**: Deadline tracking with automated escalation procedures
- **Progress Monitoring**: Real-time tracking of corrective action implementation

### Implementation Tracking
- **Milestone Tracking**: Progress monitoring against defined implementation milestones
- **Effectiveness Measurement**: Validation of corrective action effectiveness
- **Compliance Verification**: Post-implementation compliance verification and testing
- **Documentation**: Complete documentation of corrective action activities

### Integration Features
- **Compliance Analytics**: Integration with compliance monitoring and violation detection
- **Incident Response**: Coordination with incident response and breach management
- **Audit Trail**: Complete audit trail of corrective action planning and implementation
- **Reporting**: Executive reporting on corrective action status and effectiveness

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Systematic response to all compliance violations and audit findings
- ✅ Effective corrective action planning with resource allocation and timeline management
- ✅ Complete tracking and verification of remediation effectiveness
- ✅ Integration with compliance monitoring and incident response systems
