# Claims Management

## Overview
Comprehensive claims management system handling electronic claim submission, status tracking, denial management, and resubmission processes with high clean claim rates and rapid payment processing.

## Key Components

### Claim Submission
- **Electronic Submission**: EDI 837 claim submission to payers and clearinghouses
- **Batch Processing**: Efficient bulk claim submission and processing workflows
- **Real-time Validation**: Pre-submission claim scrubbing and validation
- **Multi-format Support**: Support for various claim formats and payer requirements

### Status Tracking
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Continuous claim status monitoring and updates
- **Exception Reporting**: Automated alerts for claim issues and delays
- **Payment Tracking**: Payment status and remittance advice processing
- **Aging Analysis**: Claim aging reports and follow-up prioritization

### Denial Management
- **Denial Analytics**: Systematic analysis of denial reasons and patterns
- **Automated Appeals**: Streamlined appeals process for denied claims
- **Correction Workflows**: Efficient claim correction and resubmission procedures
- **Payer Communication**: Direct communication channels with insurance payers

### Performance Optimization
- **Clean Claim Metrics**: Clean claim rate tracking and improvement initiatives
- **Turnaround Times**: Claim processing and payment turnaround analysis
- **Rejection Prevention**: Proactive rejection prevention through validation
- **Revenue Recovery**: Systematic recovery of denied and underpaid claims

## Success Criteria
- ✅ High clean claim rates (95%+) through comprehensive validation
- ✅ Rapid claim processing with minimal manual intervention
- ✅ Effective denial management with high appeal success rates
- ✅ Optimized revenue recovery through systematic claim follow-up
