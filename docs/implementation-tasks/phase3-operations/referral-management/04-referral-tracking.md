# Referral Tracking

## Overview
Comprehensive referral tracking system providing real-time visibility into referral status, outcomes, and follow-up management across the entire patient care continuum.

## Key Components

### Status Management
- **Lifecycle Tracking**: Complete referral journey from request to completion
- **Real-time Updates**: Automatic status synchronization between systems
- **Milestone Alerts**: Notifications for key referral milestones and deadlines
- **Exception Handling**: Automatic escalation for overdue or stalled referrals

### Communication Coordination
- **Multi-party Updates**: Synchronized communication between patient, referring provider, specialist
- **Automated Notifications**: Status change alerts via email, SMS, portal messages
- **Document Sharing**: Secure transfer of test results, reports, and recommendations
- **Follow-up Scheduling**: Automatic coordination of follow-up appointments

### Outcome Monitoring
- **Completion Tracking**: Verification of referral appointment attendance and completion
- **Result Integration**: Specialist reports and recommendations integration
- **Care Plan Updates**: Automatic updating of patient care plans with referral outcomes
- **Quality Metrics**: Referral success rates and patient outcome tracking

### Dashboard and Reporting
- **Provider Dashboard**: Real-time view of all active referrals by provider
- **Patient Portal**: Patient-facing referral status and appointment information
- **Administrative Reports**: Referral volume, completion rates, performance metrics
- **Compliance Tracking**: Referral management compliance and audit reporting

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Complete visibility into referral status at all times
- ✅ Automated communication and coordination between all parties
- ✅ High referral completion rates with outcome tracking
- ✅ Comprehensive reporting for quality improvement and compliance
