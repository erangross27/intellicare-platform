How it works

From a raw document to an answer the agent can act on.

Data moves through five stages — every one of them encrypted, isolated per organization, and audited.

01 · INGEST

Capture

Voice visits, uploaded PDFs, labs, and DICOM images arrive encrypted at rest.

02 · EXTRACT

Understand

A two-pass Claude Batch pipeline picks the relevant collections, then extracts the fields.

03 · STRUCTURE

Organize

Dual-path storage writes one unified record plus granular per-collection data across 884 collections.

04 · REASON

Think

The Opus 4.8 agent discovers tools via native Tool Search and chains them in an autonomous loop.

05 · DELIVER

Surface

900 templates, voice, and chat present the answer — searchable, copyable, exportable to PDF.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is IntelliCare?

IntelliCare is an AI-powered patient management system — one place for patient records, appointments, clinical documents, billing and insurance, and patient communication. A built-in Claude clinical agent can operate the whole system through plain-language conversation.

What can the AI agent actually do?

Anything the platform can do: find patients and answer questions from their records, check drug interactions and allergies before prescribing, schedule and reschedule appointments, draft and file clinical notes, interpret labs and trend vitals, verify insurance and submit claims, send patient reminders, and report on the practice — through 3,600+ functions with role-based permissions and full audit logging.

What AI model powers IntelliCare?

IntelliCare runs on Claude Opus 4.8. A custom agentic loop over the Anthropic SDK lets the clinical agent discover and chain 3,600+ tools through native Tool Search, with a 1M-token context window.

Is IntelliCare HIPAA-aligned and secure?

Yes. IntelliCare is built for healthcare compliance from the database up — HIPAA-aligned PHI handling, AES-256-GCM field-level encryption, a separate database per organization, hash-chained audit logs, RBAC with MFA, and zero-trust service accounts.

How many specialties and document types does IntelliCare cover?

IntelliCare covers 36 specialties with 900 specialty document templates and 963 print-ready PDF exports, structuring data across 884 medical collections and more than 23,000 fields.

Can IntelliCare transcribe patient visits?

Yes. A real-time voice scribe powered by ElevenLabs Scribe v2 transcribes visits live with speaker labels and an auto-structured clinical note, and it can read any agent answer aloud.
