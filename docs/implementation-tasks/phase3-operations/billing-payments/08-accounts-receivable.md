# Accounts Receivable

## Overview
Advanced accounts receivable management system optimizing cash flow through systematic collection processes, aging analysis, and automated follow-up workflows.

## Key Components

### A/R Management
- **Aging Analysis**: Comprehensive aging reports with 30, 60, 90+ day categories
- **Priority Scoring**: AI-powered collection priority ranking based on probability to pay
- **Workflow Automation**: Automated follow-up sequences and collection activities
- **Account Segmentation**: Strategic segmentation of accounts by payer type and balance

### Collection Processes
- **Automated Reminders**: Multi-channel collection reminder campaigns
- **Collection Calls**: Systematic outbound calling campaigns for overdue accounts
- **Payment Arrangements**: Flexible payment plan negotiation and setup
- **Escalation Procedures**: Progressive escalation to collection agencies and legal action

### Performance Tracking
- **Collection Metrics**: Collection rate, days in A/R, and recovery percentages
- **Staff Productivity**: Individual collector performance and efficiency metrics
- **Payer Analysis**: Insurance payer payment patterns and performance tracking
- **Account Recovery**: Recovery rates by account age, balance, and payer type

### Integration Features
- **CRM Integration**: Complete customer interaction history and communication tracking
- **Payment Processing**: Integrated payment processing for immediate collection
- **Legal Services**: Seamless handoff to legal services for difficult accounts
- **Write-off Management**: Systematic bad debt identification and write-off procedures

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Optimized collection processes reducing days in A/R by 25%
- ✅ Improved collection rates through systematic follow-up and automation
- ✅ Enhanced cash flow management with predictive collection analytics
- ✅ Comprehensive A/R performance tracking and continuous improvement
