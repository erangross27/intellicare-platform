# Competency Assessment

## Overview
Systematic competency assessment framework evaluating and validating healthcare provider skills, knowledge, and clinical performance through objective measurement tools.

## Key Components

### Assessment Framework
- **Core Competencies**: Fundamental clinical skills and knowledge requirements
- **Role-Specific Skills**: Job-specific competency requirements by healthcare role
- **Performance Metrics**: Objective measurement criteria and scoring systems
- **Continuous Evaluation**: Ongoing competency monitoring and assessment

### Assessment Methods
- **Skills Testing**: Practical demonstration of clinical skills and procedures
- **Knowledge Assessment**: Written examinations and case-based evaluations
- **Simulation Training**: High-fidelity simulation scenarios and performance evaluation
- **Peer Review**: Collaborative assessment through peer evaluation and feedback

### Performance Tracking
- **Individual Scorecards**: Personal competency tracking and development planning
- **Gap Analysis**: Identification of knowledge and skill gaps requiring development
- **Improvement Plans**: Targeted development plans for competency enhancement
- **Progress Monitoring**: Regular assessment of improvement and skill development

### Integration Features
- **Training Integration**: Automated training recommendations based on competency gaps
- **Credentialing**: Integration with professional credentialing and licensing systems
- **Performance Management**: Integration with employee performance evaluation systems
- **Reporting**: Comprehensive competency reporting for regulatory compliance

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Objective measurement of all healthcare provider competencies
- ✅ Systematic identification and remediation of competency gaps
- ✅ Continuous competency monitoring and improvement tracking
- ✅ Integration with training and professional development programs
