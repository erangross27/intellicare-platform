# Predictive Analytics

## Overview
Advanced predictive analytics platform utilizing machine learning and AI to forecast patient outcomes, resource needs, and operational performance for proactive healthcare management.

## Key Components

### Patient Risk Prediction
- **Risk Stratification**: ML models for patient risk assessment and classification
- **Readmission Prediction**: Early identification of high-risk readmission patients
- **Complication Prediction**: Predictive models for surgical and treatment complications
- **Mortality Risk**: Risk-adjusted mortality prediction and early warning systems

### Operational Forecasting
- **Demand Forecasting**: Patient volume and service demand prediction
- **Capacity Planning**: Resource allocation and staffing optimization models
- **Equipment Utilization**: Predictive maintenance and equipment replacement planning
- **Supply Chain**: Inventory optimization and supply demand forecasting

### Clinical Decision Support
- **Treatment Response**: Prediction of individual treatment response and effectiveness
- **Drug Interaction**: AI-powered drug interaction and adverse event prediction
- **Diagnostic Support**: Machine learning diagnostic assistance and pattern recognition
- **Care Pathway**: Personalized care pathway recommendations based on outcomes data

### Business Intelligence
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Predictive revenue modeling and financial planning
- **Market Analysis**: Competitive analysis and market opportunity prediction
- **Patient Retention**: Churn prediction and patient retention modeling
- **Growth Planning**: Strategic planning support through predictive modeling

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Accurate patient risk prediction enabling proactive interventions
- ✅ Optimized resource allocation through demand forecasting
- ✅ Enhanced clinical decision making through predictive insights
- ✅ Strategic planning support through business intelligence modeling
