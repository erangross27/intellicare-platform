# Revenue Cycle Management

## Overview
End-to-end revenue cycle management system optimizing financial performance through automated workflows, denial management, and comprehensive analytics.

## Key Components

### Workflow Automation
- **Charge Capture**: Automated charge entry from clinical documentation
- **Claim Scrubbing**: Pre-submission claim validation and error correction
- **Payment Posting**: Automated insurance and patient payment posting
- **Account Follow-up**: Systematic follow-up on unpaid claims and patient balances

### Denial Management
- **Denial Analytics**: Root cause analysis of claim denials and rejections
- **Automated Appeals**: Systematic appeals process for denied claims
- **Payer Communication**: Streamlined communication with insurance payers
- **Correction Workflows**: Efficient claim correction and resubmission processes

### Performance Metrics
- **Key Performance Indicators**: Days in A/R, clean claim rate, collection rate
- **Financial Dashboard**: Real-time revenue cycle performance monitoring
- **Benchmarking**: Industry comparison and performance benchmarking
- **Trend Analysis**: Historical performance trends and predictive analytics

### Accounts Receivable Management
- **Aging Reports**: Comprehensive A/R aging and collection priority reports
- **Collection Activities**: Automated and manual collection processes
- **Write-off Management**: Bad debt identification and write-off procedures
- **Recovery Services**: Integration with collection agencies and legal services

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Optimized revenue cycle with reduced days in accounts receivable
- ✅ Improved clean claim rate and faster payment processing
- ✅ Comprehensive performance tracking and continuous improvement
- ✅ Maximized collection rates through systematic follow-up and appeals
