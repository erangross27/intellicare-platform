# Policy Management System

## Overview
Centralized policy management platform maintaining, distributing, and tracking compliance with clinical policies, procedures, and healthcare regulations.

## Key Components

### Policy Repository
- **Digital Library**: Comprehensive library of clinical policies and procedures
- **Version Control**: Systematic version management with change tracking
- **Search Functionality**: Advanced search and filtering capabilities for policy retrieval
- **Access Control**: Role-based access to appropriate policies by user type

### Policy Lifecycle
- **Development Process**: Structured policy creation and review workflows
- **Approval Workflows**: Multi-level approval process with stakeholder review
- **Publication**: Systematic policy distribution and notification
- **Review Cycles**: Regular policy review and update scheduling

### Implementation Integration
- **Service**: `policyManagementService.js` - Complete policy lifecycle management
- **Document Management**: Integration with document management and storage systems
- **Training Integration**: Policy training requirements and completion tracking
- **Audit Trail**: Complete policy access and modification audit logging

### Compliance Features
- **Acknowledgment Tracking**: Staff policy acknowledgment and compliance confirmation
- **Training Requirements**: Automated training assignment based on policy changes
- **Exception Reporting**: Identification and tracking of policy compliance exceptions
- **Regulatory Alignment**: Policy alignment with healthcare regulations and standards

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Centralized management of all clinical policies and procedures
- ✅ Automated policy distribution with staff acknowledgment tracking
- ✅ Regular policy review and update cycles with version control
- ✅ Complete compliance monitoring and exception reporting
