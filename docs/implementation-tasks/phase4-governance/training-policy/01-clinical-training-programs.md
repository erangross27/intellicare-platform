# Clinical Training Programs

## Overview
Comprehensive clinical training and continuing education program ensuring healthcare providers maintain current medical knowledge, skills, and certifications.

## Key Components

### Core Training Curriculum
- **Medical Protocols**: Evidence-based clinical protocols and treatment guidelines
- **Emergency Procedures**: Critical care and emergency response training
- **Technology Training**: EHR system, medical devices, and clinical software training
- **Quality Improvement**: Clinical quality metrics and improvement methodologies

### Certification Management
- **License Tracking**: Professional license renewal and compliance monitoring
- **Board Certifications**: Specialty board certification tracking and renewal
- **CME Credits**: Continuing Medical Education credit tracking and reporting
- **Competency Assessment**: Clinical competency validation and skill assessment

### Implementation Integration
- **Service**: `securityTrainingService.js` - Extended for clinical training management
- **Models**: `TrainingRecord.js` - Clinical training history and certification tracking
- **Scheduling**: Automated training reminders and compliance monitoring
- **Multi-language**: Hebrew and English training materials for Israeli market

### Training Delivery
- **Interactive Modules**: Engaging clinical simulation and case-based training
- **Video Conferences**: Remote training capabilities for continuing education
- **Hands-on Practice**: Practical skill development and simulation training
- **Assessment Tools**: Competency testing and knowledge validation

## Success Criteria
- ✅ 100% provider completion of required clinical training
- ✅ Automated license and certification tracking system
- ✅ Current medical knowledge through continuing education programs
- ✅ Competency-based training with practical skill validation
