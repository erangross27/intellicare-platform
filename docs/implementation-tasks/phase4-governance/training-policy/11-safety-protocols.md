# Safety Protocols

## Overview
Comprehensive safety protocol system ensuring patient safety, staff safety, and regulatory compliance through systematic safety procedures and training programs.

## Key Components

### Patient Safety Protocols
- **Medication Safety**: Safe prescribing, dispensing, and administration protocols
- **Infection Control**: Comprehensive infection prevention and control procedures
- **Fall Prevention**: Patient fall risk assessment and prevention protocols
- **Emergency Response**: Medical emergency response procedures and protocols

### Occupational Safety
- **Workplace Safety**: Staff safety procedures and hazard prevention
- **Personal Protective Equipment**: PPE requirements and proper usage training
- **Hazardous Materials**: Safe handling of medical and chemical hazards
- **Ergonomics**: Workplace ergonomics and injury prevention

### Safety Training
- **Mandatory Training**: Required safety training for all staff categories
- **Competency Validation**: Practical demonstration of safety procedures
- **Regular Updates**: Ongoing safety training and protocol updates
- **Emergency Drills**: Regular emergency response drills and training

### Incident Management
- **Reporting Systems**: Comprehensive safety incident reporting and tracking
- **Investigation Procedures**: Systematic investigation of safety incidents
- **Corrective Actions**: Implementation of corrective actions to prevent recurrence
- **Learning Culture**: Promoting a culture of safety learning and improvement

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Comprehensive safety protocol implementation and compliance
- ✅ Zero preventable patient safety incidents
- ✅ Full staff competency in safety procedures and protocols
- ✅ Continuous safety improvement through incident learning and analysis
