# Copay Collection

## Overview
Streamlined copay collection system maximizing point-of-service collections through automated eligibility verification, payment processing, and patient communication tools.

## Key Components

### Point-of-Service Collection
- **Real-time Verification**: Instant copay amount verification at check-in
- **Payment Processing**: Multiple payment method support at service delivery
- **Collection Alerts**: Staff alerts for outstanding copay obligations
- **Exception Handling**: Systematic handling of copay collection exceptions

### Patient Communication
- **Pre-visit Notifications**: Copay amount communication before appointment
- **Financial Responsibility**: Clear communication of patient financial obligations
- **Payment Options**: Multiple payment method education and setup
- **Hardship Programs**: Financial assistance program information and enrollment

### Collection Workflows
- **Automated Reminders**: Post-visit copay collection reminder campaigns
- **Payment Plans**: Flexible copay payment plan options for patients
- **Collection Tracking**: Systematic tracking of copay collection efforts
- **Recovery Processes**: Escalated collection procedures for unpaid copays

### Analytics and Reporting
- **Collection Metrics**: Copay collection rates and performance tracking
- **Staff Performance**: Individual staff copay collection effectiveness
- **Trend Analysis**: Copay collection trends and improvement opportunities
- **Financial Impact**: Revenue impact analysis of copay collection optimization

## Success Criteria
- ✅ High point-of-service collection rates reducing administrative overhead
- ✅ Clear patient communication improving payment compliance
- ✅ Automated collection processes maximizing copay recovery
- ✅ Performance tracking enabling continuous collection improvement
