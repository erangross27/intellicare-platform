# Quality Metrics

## Overview
Comprehensive quality measurement and reporting system tracking clinical quality indicators, patient safety metrics, and continuous improvement initiatives.

## Key Components

### Clinical Quality Indicators
- **Core Measures**: CMS core measures and quality reporting requirements
- **Patient Safety Indicators**: AHRQ patient safety indicators and sentinel events
- **Clinical Outcomes**: Mortality, morbidity, readmission, and infection rates
- **Process Measures**: Adherence to clinical protocols and evidence-based care

### Patient Experience Metrics
- **Satisfaction Scores**: Patient satisfaction surveys and experience metrics
- **Communication Quality**: Provider-patient communication effectiveness
- **Access Metrics**: Appointment availability and wait time analysis
- **Discharge Experience**: Discharge planning and follow-up effectiveness

### Quality Improvement Analytics
- **Trend Analysis**: Quality metric trends and improvement tracking
- **Benchmark Comparison**: Comparison with national and peer benchmarks
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Statistical analysis of quality variations
- **Improvement Impact**: Measurement of quality improvement initiative effectiveness

### Regulatory Compliance
- **Accreditation Metrics**: Joint Commission and other accreditation requirements
- **Quality Reporting**: CMS quality reporting and value-based payments
- **Certification Maintenance**: Quality metrics for certification compliance
- **Public Reporting**: Transparency reporting for quality metrics

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Comprehensive quality measurement across all clinical services
- ✅ Real-time quality monitoring with early warning systems
- ✅ Evidence-based quality improvement initiatives and tracking
- ✅ Full regulatory compliance and public quality reporting
