# Allergy & Immunology Platform Roadmap
## Journey from A- to A+ Medical Platform

**Medical Specialty:** Allergy & Immunology
**Current Grade:** A- (Excellent clinical intelligence, room for workflow execution)
**Target Grade:** A+ (Complete closed-loop care platform)

---

## 📁 Structure

This roadmap is organized into **28 enhancement areas** across 4 tiers:

### 🔴 TIER 1: CRITICAL TOOLS (10 tasks)
Foundation for closing the loop on patient care

### 🟡 TIER 2: ADVANCED AI (6 tasks)
Predictive analytics and personalization

### 🟢 TIER 3: PATIENT ENGAGEMENT (6 tasks)
Patient-facing tools and mobile integration

### 🔵 TIER 4: POPULATION HEALTH (6 tasks)
Quality metrics and research capabilities
