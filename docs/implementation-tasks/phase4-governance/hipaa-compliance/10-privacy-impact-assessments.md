# Privacy Impact Assessments

## Overview
Systematic privacy impact assessment framework evaluating PHI handling procedures, identifying risks, and implementing appropriate safeguards for HIPAA compliance.

## Key Components

### Assessment Framework
- **Risk Identification**: Systematic identification of privacy risks and vulnerabilities
- **Impact Analysis**: Assessment of potential harm from privacy violations
- **Mitigation Strategies**: Development of risk reduction and control measures
- **Compliance Validation**: Verification of regulatory requirement adherence

### Assessment Scope
- **System Assessments**: Privacy evaluation of all PHI-handling systems
- **Process Reviews**: Analysis of clinical and administrative workflows
- **Vendor Evaluations**: Third-party privacy and security assessments
- **Change Impact**: Privacy assessment for system and process changes

### Implementation Integration
- **Compliance Analytics**: Integration with `complianceAnalyticsService.js`
- **Risk Scoring**: Automated privacy risk calculation and reporting
- **Documentation**: Systematic PIA documentation and tracking
- **Remediation**: Integration with corrective action planning

### Assessment Process
- **Automated Screening**: System-generated privacy risk assessments
- **Expert Review**: Privacy officer evaluation and approval
- **Stakeholder Input**: Multi-disciplinary assessment team participation
- **Continuous Monitoring**: Ongoing privacy risk evaluation and updates

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Comprehensive privacy risk identification and mitigation
- ✅ Systematic assessment of all PHI-handling activities
- ✅ Integration with overall compliance monitoring system
- ✅ Proactive privacy protection through impact assessment
