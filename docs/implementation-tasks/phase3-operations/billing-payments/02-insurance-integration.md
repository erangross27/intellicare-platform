# Insurance Integration

## Overview
Comprehensive insurance management system providing real-time eligibility verification, benefit checking, and automated claims processing with major insurance networks.

## Key Components

### Eligibility Verification
- **Real-time Checks**: Instant insurance eligibility verification at appointment scheduling
- **Benefit Information**: Coverage details, copays, deductibles, and authorization requirements
- **Multi-payer Support**: Integration with major insurance carriers and government programs
- **Batch Verification**: Bulk eligibility checking for scheduled appointments

### Claims Processing
- **Electronic Submission**: Direct EDI submission to insurance payers and clearinghouses
- **Status Tracking**: Real-time claim status monitoring and update notifications
- **Remittance Processing**: Automatic ERA (Electronic Remittance Advice) processing
- **Exception Handling**: Automated handling of claim rejections and denials

### Prior Authorization
- **Authorization Tracking**: Integration with insurance prior authorization systems
- **Approval Workflows**: Automated authorization request and approval tracking
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Ensuring services are properly authorized before delivery
- **Appeal Management**: Systematic appeals process for denied authorizations

### Network Management
- **Provider Enrollment**: Insurance network participation tracking and maintenance
- **Contract Management**: Payer contract terms, fee schedules, and reimbursement rates
- **Credentialing Support**: Provider credentialing status and renewal tracking
- **Performance Analytics**: Payer performance metrics and reimbursement analysis

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Real-time insurance verification reducing claim denials by 60%
- ✅ Automated claims processing with 24-48 hour submission turnaround
- ✅ Comprehensive prior authorization management preventing service delays
- ✅ Optimized payer relationships through performance tracking and analysis
