# Performance Management

## Overview
Comprehensive performance management system providing continuous feedback, development planning, and performance evaluation for all healthcare staff.

## Key Components

### Performance Evaluation
- **Regular Reviews**: Structured performance reviews with standardized criteria
- **360-Degree Feedback**: Multi-source feedback from supervisors, peers, and patients
- **Clinical Metrics**: Objective performance measurement through quality indicators
- **Goal Setting**: Individual performance goals aligned with organizational objectives

### Development Planning
- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Identification of competency gaps and development opportunities
- **Career Pathways**: Clear career progression paths and advancement opportunities
- **Training Recommendations**: Personalized training and development recommendations
- **Mentoring Programs**: Professional mentoring and career guidance support

### Performance Tracking
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Continuous performance monitoring and feedback
- **Key Performance Indicators**: Objective measurement of individual and team performance
- **Dashboard Analytics**: Performance analytics and trend identification
- **Improvement Plans**: Structured performance improvement planning and support

### Recognition and Incentives
- **Achievement Recognition**: Formal recognition programs for exceptional performance
- **Professional Development**: Investment in continuing education and skill development
- **Career Advancement**: Merit-based promotion and career advancement opportunities
- **Performance Incentives**: Aligned incentive programs supporting organizational goals

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Fair and objective performance evaluation system
- ✅ Effective development planning and career progression support
- ✅ Continuous performance improvement through feedback and coaching
- ✅ High staff engagement and retention through recognition and development
