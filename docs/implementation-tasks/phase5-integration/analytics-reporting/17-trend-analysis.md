# Trend Analysis

## Overview
Advanced trend analysis platform providing statistical trend identification, pattern recognition, and forecasting capabilities for strategic planning and proactive management.

## Key Components

### Statistical Trend Analysis
- **Time Series Analysis**: Advanced time series analysis and decomposition techniques
- **Seasonal Patterns**: Identification and analysis of seasonal variations and cycles
- **Moving Averages**: Trend smoothing and moving average analysis
- **Regression Analysis**: Linear and non-linear trend regression modeling

### Pattern Recognition
- **Trend Classification**: Automatic classification of trend types and patterns
- **Anomaly Detection**: Identification of unusual trends and pattern deviations
- **Cycle Analysis**: Business cycle and periodic pattern identification
- **Correlation Trends**: Multi-variable trend correlation and relationship analysis

### Forecasting Models
- **Short-term Forecasting**: Near-term trend projection and prediction
- **Long-term Forecasting**: Strategic planning horizon trend forecasting
- **Confidence Intervals**: Statistical confidence measures for trend predictions
- **Scenario Analysis**: Multiple scenario trend modeling and analysis

### Trend Visualization
- **Trend Charts**: Advanced charting with trend lines and statistical indicators
- **Interactive Analysis**: Interactive trend exploration and drill-down capabilities
- **Comparative Trends**: Multi-series trend comparison and analysis
- **Trend Dashboards**: Executive dashboard for key trend monitoring

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Accurate trend identification and pattern recognition across all organizational metrics
- ✅ Reliable forecasting supporting strategic planning and resource allocation
- ✅ Early identification of emerging trends enabling proactive management
- ✅ Visual trend analysis supporting intuitive understanding and decision-making
