# Continuing Education

## Overview
Comprehensive continuing education program ensuring healthcare providers maintain current knowledge and skills through accredited educational activities and professional development.

## Key Components

### Educational Programs
- **CME Courses**: Continuing Medical Education programs with accredited credits
- **Online Learning**: Interactive e-learning modules and virtual conferences
- **Professional Conferences**: External conference attendance and credit tracking
- **Internal Education**: In-house training sessions and case presentations

### Credit Management
- **Credit Tracking**: Automated tracking of continuing education credits by provider
- **Requirement Monitoring**: Individual CME requirements based on specialty and license type
- **Deadline Alerts**: Automated reminders for upcoming credit renewal deadlines
- **Compliance Reporting**: CME compliance reporting for licensing boards and accreditation

### Program Administration
- **Course Catalog**: Comprehensive catalog of available educational programs
- **Registration System**: Online registration and enrollment management
- **Certificate Management**: Digital certificate generation and storage
- **Evaluation System**: Course evaluation and feedback collection

### Quality Assurance
- **Accreditation Standards**: Ensuring all programs meet accreditation requirements
- **Outcome Measurement**: Learning outcome assessment and effectiveness measurement
- **Provider Feedback**: Regular feedback collection and program improvement
- **Content Currency**: Regular content review and update to maintain relevance

## Success Criteria
- ✅ 100% provider compliance with continuing education requirements
- ✅ Automated credit tracking and compliance monitoring
- ✅ High-quality accredited educational programs
- ✅ Continuous program improvement based on outcome measurement
