# Onboarding Orientation

## Overview
Comprehensive new employee onboarding program ensuring rapid integration, regulatory compliance, and effective job performance for all healthcare staff.

## Key Components

### Orientation Curriculum
- **Organization Overview**: IntelliCare mission, values, and organizational structure
- **Role-Specific Training**: Job-specific responsibilities, procedures, and expectations
- **System Training**: EHR systems, clinical software, and technology platforms
- **Compliance Requirements**: Mandatory regulatory and safety training completion

### Pre-boarding Preparation
- **Digital Onboarding**: Online completion of administrative requirements
- **Document Preparation**: License verification and credentialing documentation
- **System Access**: Account creation and security clearance processing
- **Schedule Coordination**: Training schedule and mentor assignment

### Structured Timeline
- **Week 1**: Basic orientation, safety training, and system access
- **Week 2-4**: Role-specific training and clinical competency validation
- **30-60-90 Days**: Progressive responsibility increase with mentorship support
- **First Year**: Continuing education and performance milestone tracking

### Support Systems
- **Mentorship Program**: Experienced staff mentor assignment and support
- **Progress Tracking**: Individual onboarding progress monitoring and reporting
- **Feedback Mechanisms**: Regular feedback collection and program improvement
- **Performance Integration**: Connection to ongoing performance management systems

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Streamlined onboarding process reducing time-to-productivity
- ✅ 100% completion of mandatory training and compliance requirements
- ✅ Effective integration with mentorship and support systems
- ✅ High new employee satisfaction and retention rates
