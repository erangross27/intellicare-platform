# Cost Tracking

## Overview
Comprehensive cost tracking and management system providing detailed visibility into operational expenses, resource utilization, and profitability analysis for informed financial decision-making.

## Key Components

### Expense Management
- **Cost Categories**: Systematic categorization of all operational expenses
- **Department Allocation**: Cost allocation to specific departments and service lines
- **Resource Tracking**: Personnel, equipment, and supply cost monitoring
- **Variable Cost Analysis**: Variable vs. fixed cost identification and management

### Resource Utilization
- **Staff Productivity**: Provider and staff productivity metrics and cost analysis
- **Equipment ROI**: Medical equipment utilization and return on investment tracking
- **Facility Costs**: Space utilization and facility cost per service analysis
- **Supply Chain**: Medical supply usage tracking and cost optimization

### Profitability Analysis
- **Service Profitability**: Profit margin analysis by service type and procedure
- **Patient Profitability**: Individual patient and payer profitability assessment
- **Department Performance**: Department-level profit and loss analysis
- **Activity-Based Costing**: Detailed cost allocation based on actual resource consumption

### Budget Management
- **Budget Planning**: Annual and quarterly budget development and tracking
- **Variance Analysis**: Actual vs. budget variance identification and analysis
- **Cost Control**: Automated alerts for budget overruns and cost exceptions
- **Forecasting**: Predictive cost modeling and budget forecasting tools

## Success Criteria
- ✅ Comprehensive visibility into all operational costs and resource utilization
- ✅ Data-driven cost optimization and efficiency improvement initiatives
- ✅ Accurate profitability analysis supporting strategic decision making
- ✅ Effective budget management with variance tracking and control measures
